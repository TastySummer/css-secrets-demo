Flexible background positioning 灵活的背景定位

Extended background-position solution

background: url(img/water.svg) no-repeat bottom right #CEE2EF;
background-position: right 20px bottom 10px;

background-origin solution

padding: 20px;
background: url("img/water.svg") no-repeat #CEE2EF bottom right; /* or 100% 100% */
background-origin: content-box;

calc() solution

background: url("img/water.svg") no-repeat #CEE2EF;
background-position: calc(100% - 20px) calc(100% - 20px);
